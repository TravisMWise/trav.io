Pre-College

  Before I came back to college in 2018, I had taken 4 years off of education. I graduated high school in 2014 from Deland High and never expected to go to college at any point in my life. I did however have a very successful time in high school with being apart of the engineering program for 3 of the 4 years and did dual enrollment in my fourth year taking most of my classes at Daytona State. Nevertheless, I decided to continue working the job I had at the time which was an entry level job at Publix Supermarkets and then move on to a higher paying job at Command Medical Products working as an extrusion operator making high quality medical tubing. After three years at Publix and four years at Command I realized that that type of job was not what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I looked inward to see what I would enjoy and what I have passion in. After researching and trying new things for months I decided to go back to school and go into Computer Science. I have always enjoyed math and the little bit of coding I had done in the past came easily and was fun, not to mention the field is growing and has lots of potential.
